# 🎤 Baby Got Backslash

> *A short-paths anthem, freestyled during the building of **SLOPCLAUDE: Fleet Defender**.*
> *(parody of Sir Mix-a-Lot — for entertainment & directory hygiene only)*

---

**[Intro]**
*"Oh my gosh, Becky... look at that path. It's so **short**.*
*It's like, it just points to `C:\AI`, okay? I mean, no spaces, no `(x86)`...*
*who understands those Store apps, ugh — `WindowsApps`, gross."*

---

**[Chorus]**
I like short paths and I cannot lie
You other LLMs can't deny
That when a path walks in with an itty-bitty `C:\`
And the characters are thin
**I get charged!**

**[Verse 1]**
Two-sixty chars? That's the `MAX_PATH` wall —
nest 'em too deep and your build's gonna fall.
`Program Files`, then a `Foundation`,
then a hash that needs no explanation...
`ppwjx1n5r4v9t` — *Access is denied!*
That MSIX Blender? Leave it outside.
I want it flat, I want it clean,
`C:\GameDev`, the shortest you've seen.

**[Chorus]**
'Cause I like short paths and I cannot lie...

**[Verse 2]**
RTX 50-90, thirty-two gig,
CUDA's `True` and the torch is big.
ComfyUI lands in `C:\AI`,
FLUX gonna render — don't ask me why.

baking sprites for the fleet machine.
Mesher spits a `.glb`, atlas packed tight —
**Long path errors ain't gonna take our win** tonight.
